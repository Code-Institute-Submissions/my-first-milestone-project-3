MTB & Gravel Bike Tours

Catalonia & Pyrenees

Day Tours

Stay in luxury accommodation and venture out on day trips of varying distances to suit. Ride to remote villages for a leisurely lunch and return to your lodgings for a swim, evening sundowners and a sumptuous meal!

Ride the Pirinexus

Ride the Pirinexus! A circular route of 360km, which shares coastal and mountain views in Spain and France. The route can be completed in 4 - 7 days at your leisure, either camping or 3* and 4* accomodation.

MTB Bike-Pack

Hit out into the mountains in Spain and France. Bike up Canigou, the second highest mountain in the Pyrenees, visit historical ruins, camp out amongst the stars and enjoy the view across the landscape below!

About
